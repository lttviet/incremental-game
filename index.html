Intro

You are fresh out of college and you have a big dream: to build the best todo list app. It will motivate people to get things done not only daily but also their lifelong goals. Millions will achieve their goals (learn a foregin language, draw a portrait, go to the gym,...). And you will hopefully make enough sales to pay back the college debt.

There is one problem though, you are out of money. So, try to take any side job that you can get to get the lights on while working on the app during your free time.

Good luck, mate.
